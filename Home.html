Health Calendar

Health calendar is a web application developed for all who compose of the Thomasian community. It shows the availability of the doctors on hand and allows patients to have a scheduled appointment with them. This web application is done for time optimization of both the doctors and patients. Instead of waiting in a long line, the patient can arrive at the health service on the time of his appointment.

The navigations on the left side will redirect you to different parts of thie web application.
About Us describes what is the UST Health Service.
Services Offered lists the services available to all Thomasian that the health service provides.
Appointment is the page where one can see the availability of the doctors and can set an appointment.
Contact Us offers a section where one who has availed of the services can send their feedbacks, comments or remarks.
